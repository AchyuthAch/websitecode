Why Choose Us

24X7 Care

Navjeevan Hospital provides 24x7 medical care to all patients as well as to emergency care.

Affordability

Navjeevan Hospital is very cost effective. Medicine available in the pharmacy are good quality and less costly.Hospital bed charges,investigation,procedures are very reasonable.People of any socioeconomic class can afford quality treatment here.

Good,Clean Environment

Navjeevan Hospital is located in a place surrounded by greenary,tea garden all around,away from city polution & noise.Atmosphere is clean and serene which refreshes mind,body & soul.

Ambulance Facility

24x7 round the clock well equipped ambulance is available.

Qualified Medical & Paramedical Staff

The Medical & paramedical staffs in the department are highly qualified & experienced. The consultants are supported by qualified resident doctors and nurses to provide round the clock supportive care to patients.

24x7 Intensive Care & Operation theatre

Navjeevan Hospital provides 24x7 Intensive care .Operation theatre is available 24 hours as per requirments.

Emergency support

Emergency is open 24 hours on all the days.Because of its proximity to NH 37 many road traffic accident cases come to this hospital.Severe head injury cases which require neurosurgical intervention are referred to nearby neurosurgical centre.

About Us

Navjeevan Hospital & Rural Health Care Centre Gayaganga is the only hospital run by the F.C Sisters, besides primary Health Centres in Basanti,Dundajhar and Hatighisa.The mission of this hospital is to make health services available to common people at an affordable cost.

Navjeevan Hospital always plunged into various community health programmes.It has also been training village health workers as well as assistant nurses.This has brought a lot of health awareness in the community.
