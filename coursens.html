Course:

GNM

Duration:

The duration of GNM course is 3 years.

Admission Requirements:

  • Unmarried girls between the age 17-27 years.
  • Should have passed 10+2 with a minimum of 40% marks.
  • Should have fair knowledge of English language.
  • Should be medically fit.

    • Policies for Admission:

  • Admission is open to candidates who have studied in the West Bengal State, and the students from the other states should have residential certificates from West Bengal.
  • The course starts in the month of September. Application forms and prospectus may be obtained from the office of Navjeevan School of Nursing, Gayaganga, P.O. Kamala Bagan, Dist. Darjeeling on payment of Rs. 250/- either cash or Demand Draft address to the Navjeevan School of Nursing.
  • Duly filled in application forms should reach the office by the prescribed date together with a stamped self-addressed envelope.
  • The application form should be filled by the applicant in her own handwriting with the singnature of the Parent/Guardian.

    • Application Form:

  • Application Form is issued in the month of June.

    • Selection:

  • Candidates provisionally selected on the basis of the merit of the qualifying exams are called for a written test and interview at Navjeevan School of Nursing.
  • Date and time for written test and interview will be communicated in due course.
  • All the candidate appearing for the interview should bring their original certificates.
  • Preference will be given to tribal students.

    • Eligibility for Admission to examination:

  • As per the policy of the Nursing Council, the pass percentage for all examinations and tests is 50. Unless this percentage is attained in every subject the student will not be eligible to sit for West Bengal Nursing Council examination.
  • She has to complete minimum eleven months of the course every year.
  • She should complete the record of practical experience.
